 The Privacy Statement for Students

Government Science College Bangalore (hereinafter called ‘the College’) intends to make it known to all its
 Students who visit the web site that the College may collect personal information about them, either directly
 (where they are asked to provide the information) or indirectly and that such information will be kept secure
 and used only as our Students would want us to, since our most important asset is our Students' trust. Hence,
 the College will only use these personal data in accordance with the purposes set forth in this Privacy
 Statement and is committed to safeguarding the personal information collected.  

 
1.      The College will safeguard, according to strict standards of security and confidentiality, any information
 our Students share with us.

2.      The College will limit the collection and use of customer information to the minimum required to deliver
 superior service to our Students, that includes advising our Students about our products, services and other
 opportunities, and to administer our business.

3.      The College will permit only authorized employees, who are trained in the proper handling of customer
 information, to have access to that information. Employees who violate our Privacy Statement will be subject

to our normal disciplinary process.

4.      The College will not reveal customer information to any external organization unless we have previously
 informed the customer in disclosures or agreements, or authorized by the customer, or are required by law. 

The College disclaims any liability arising from use or disclosure of information to any external organization.

5.      The College will always maintain control over the confidentiality of our customer information. We may,
 however, facilitate relevant offers from reputable companies. These companies are not permitted to retain any
 customer information unless the customer has specifically expressed interest in their products or services.


6.      The College may inform Students, on their requests, at least once annually, how they may remove their
 names from marketing lists. At any time, Students may contact us to remove their names from such lists.

7.      Whenever the College hires other organizations to provide support services, the College will require
 them to conform to our policy standards and to allow us to audit them for compliance.

8.      The College will not use or share - internally or externally - personally identifiable medical
 information for any purpose other than the underwriting or administration of a customer's policy, claim or
 account, or as disclosed to the customer when the information is collected, or to which the customer consents.

9.      The College will attempt to keep customer files complete, up to date, and accurate. We will inform
 Students how and where to conveniently access their account information (except when we are prohibited by law),
 and how to notify us about errors, which we will promptly correct.

10.      The College will continuously make the assessment to ensure that customer privacy is respected.

 

Internet Privacy Policy

Personally Identifiable Information ("Information") means information that is collected through web site 
operated by the College and how it may later be disclosed.

 

 The College endeavors to safeguard and ensure the security of the information provided by the Customer.

 encryption, the Students' system (if configured accordingly) will display an appropriate message ensuring the

The Customer would be required to cooperate with the College in order to ensure the security of the information,
