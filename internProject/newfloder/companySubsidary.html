About the company

Erwin & Werner Systems

Erwin & Werner Systems, or E&W Systems, is a firm dedicated to constructing India's first quantum computer. E&W Systems is named after two of the greatest physicists of all time who dedicated their lives to quantum mechanics.

On the 27th of November, 2017, M.K Yogesh, the founder, gave birth to E&W Systems with the idea of a quantum computer and its possibilities in critical domains such as defence and health care systems. E&W Systems came to life in March, 2022.
