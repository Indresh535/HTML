Security Tip to avoid Phishing attacks


Security Tip to avoid Phishing attacks : College Authority never ask their customers to provide their username, password, or credit card numbers through e-mails. User ID and password should not be parted with for any purpose other than transaction. Never click any link given inside the e-mail message. Directly type the URL of your college in address field of browser. If you do not know the URL of our college's website, take time to call the college immediately to find out. Our college's URL is as under : https://gfgc.kar.nic.in/gsc/ https://gfgc.kar.nic.in/gsc/ Check the language and spelling of the text contained in the e-mail. If you find misspelled words or substandard language, conclude that it is not from college. If the e-mail urges to act immediately without delay, failing which your account will be closed down may be a tool to create panic. Never provide your personal information to anybody, come what may. college will never seek Log-In, Password or any personal information on Phone or e-mail. Do not respond to such request. These may be phishing traps. Various instances of financial frauds are reported in the country on account of fake phone numbers published by fraudulent persons in various fake websites as the customer care number of College Authority / Financial Institutions. It seems that fraudulent customer care number is being shown to the public on Internet Search Engines, which are misleading people and making them vulnerable to financial frauds. In order to avoid such frauds, Customers / Public are requested not to depend on the contact numbers obtained through internet search for any collegeing activities with Government Science College. The contact numbers published on college's official website https://gfgc.kar.nic.in/gsc/ should only be used for contacting the college in case of need. Also to ensure that confidential information like user id, password, PIN, OTP, credit/debit card details etc. are not to be provided / disclosed to anybody.
